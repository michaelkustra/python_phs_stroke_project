What is the most common stroke diagnosis in Scotland?

Assumptions

Our data contains four types of diagnosis in each data set:

  • Cerebrovascular Disease
  • Stroke
  • Subarachnoid Haemorrhage
  • TIAs and related syndromes

For the purposes of this question I will categorise the Subarachnoid Haemorrhage and TIAs and related syndromes as the two main types of stroke to be analysed.
